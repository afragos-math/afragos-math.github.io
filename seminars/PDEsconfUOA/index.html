Overview

This the webpage for 2025s conference in Partial Differential Equations.

The aim of this conference is to bring together researchers and students, to foster discussions and to promote collaborations in the field of Partial Differential Equations.

It will take place in Athens, in the historical building of National and Kapodistrian University of Athens, Panepistimiou 30. You can check the INFO section for the exact location.

You can find all confirmed speakers in the SPEAKERS List section. The timetable can be found in the PROGRAM section
