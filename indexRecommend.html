❡ Book Recommendations

In English:

  • A Course in Abstract Harmonic Analysis, Gerald B. Folland (CRC Press)
  • Algebraic Topology, Allen Hatcher (Cornell Univ.)
  • An Introduction to Differential Geometry and Riemmanian Manifolds, William M. Boothby (Academic Press)
  • Analysis on Manifolds, James Munkres (Addison-Wesley Publishing Company)
  • Differential Geometry, Kühnel Wolfgang (AMS, Student Mathematical Library Vol. 77)
  • Elliptic Systems of Phase Transition Type, Nicholas D. Alikakos, Giorgio Fusco, Panayotis Smyrnelis (Birkhäuser)
  • Fractals in Probability and Analysis, Bishop Christopher, Peres Yuval (Cambridge University Press)
  • Hyperbolic Geometry, Brice Loustau
  • Geometry in a Fréchet Context, C. T. Dodson, G. Galanis, E. Vassiliou (Cambridge University Press)
  • Introduction to Riemannian Manifolds, John M. Lee (Springer)
  • Mathematical Game Theory, Ulrich Faigle (World Scientific)
  • Measure Theory and the Fine Properties of Functions, Evans L.C., Gariepy R.F. (CRC Press)
  • Measure, Topology, and Fractal Geometry, Gerald Edgar (Springer)
  • Optimal Transport for Applied Mathematicians, Filippo Santambrogio (Birkhäuser)
  • Partial Differential Equations, Evans Lawrence C. (AMS)
  • Quantum Physics, Steven Casiorowicz (John Wiley & Sons)
  • Quantum Theory for Mathematicians, Brian C. Hall (Springer)
  • Real Analysis, Gerald B. Folland (John Wiley & Sons)

  • Stable Solutions of Elliptic Partial Differential Equations, Louis Dupaigne (CRC Press)
  • Tilings and Patterns, Grunbaum B., Shephard G.C. (W.H. Freeman and Company New York)
